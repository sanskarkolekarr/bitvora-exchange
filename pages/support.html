Frequently Asked Questions

How long does payout take? expand_more
After the blockchain confirms your transaction, our administrative team requires an average of 15 minutes to manually execute the INR payout. During extreme congestion this might spike up to an hour, but it is typically fully settled within the timeframe displayed on the status dashboard.
What confirmations are required per chain? expand_more
Each network dictates finality requirements based on consensus parameters. Bitcoin requires 2 confirmations, Ethereum 12, Binance Smart Chain 15, and Tron 19. Solana and TON achieve near-instant finality after 1 block. Litecoin requires 3 confirmations.
What if I sent from a centralized exchange? expand_more
We strongly advise against this. If you initiated the send from Binance, OKX, or Bybit directly to our deposit address, their cold/hot wallet bridging logic obfuscates your origin and bundles your request. The engine won't be able to map a solitary TXID. You will need to open a support ticket immediately.
What if I entered the wrong bank account? expand_more
The payout team checks the recipient metadata via bank validators before dispatching IMPS. If the account details don't match, they pause your workflow and flag your transaction status as failed. Open a support ticket referencing your TXID to provide correct details and retrieve your funds.
What if my transaction is stuck in verifying? expand_more
This indicates either you submitted a mempool transaction that is being dropped by miners due to low gas, or you've provided the incorrect TXID mismatching the actual chain hash. Give it 20 minutes, then contact support if the problem persists.
Can I cancel a transaction? expand_more
You can safely navigate away from an unverified pending lock if you decide not to send the tokens. However, once you broadcast your crypto and bind the TXID, the transaction becomes irreversible due to blockchain constraints.
What is the platform fee? expand_more
BITVORA Exchange does not apply withdrawal fees. The exchange quote rate inherently accommodates the backend operational and bridging threshold margins. There are no surprise final calculation hits to your portfolio.
How is the INR rate calculated? expand_more
Our internal indexers fetch the global USD average for the selected token and cross-correlate against real-time OTC trading desk Rupee rates, bypassing local P2P risk premiums.
