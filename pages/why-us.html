01

No Account. No Login.

The fastest path to INR.

Unlike every other exchange in the Indian market, BITVORA requires zero registration. No email address, no phone number, no KYC documents, no waiting for approval, no identity verification. There is no account concept on this platform.

The user arrives, selects a token, gets a deposit address, sends crypto, submits a TXID, enters their bank account, and receives INR. The entire interaction takes under two minutes from start to submission. Zero friction by design.

No data is stored beyond what is strictly necessary to process the transaction. There is no user profile, no history associated with your identity, and no database of your behavior. Each transaction is its own isolated record.

02

Direct RPC Verification.

No oracles. No trust assumptions.

Every TXID submitted to BITVORA is verified by querying blockchain RPC nodes directly. The backend does not call a third-party block explorer API — it connects to the chain itself. Etherscan, Tronscan, Solscan, and similar services are not involved in the verification path.

The recipient address is read from the raw transaction data. The token amount is verified against the vault deposit address. The confirmation count is checked against chain-specific thresholds. Every single one of these checks comes from a live read against the actual blockchain node, not from a cached or third-party data source.

This means if the chain confirms it happened, it happened. There is no intermediary that could be compromised, cached incorrectly, or return stale data. The system is as close to trustless verification as a manual payout workflow allows.

15-Minute Average Payout.

Fastest off-ramp in the Indian market.

The typical flow timing: transaction detected within seconds of its first confirmation on-chain. Verification completes in one to two minutes depending on the chain's block time. Payout queued immediately after verification passes. INR transferred via IMPS or UPI typically within ten to twenty minutes.

Live platform average: AVG: ~15 MIN

Compare this to centralized Indian exchanges: KYC verification queues, withdrawal processing windows, bank cut-off times, and T+1 settlement cycles commonly add hours or full business days to what should be a simple transfer. BITVORA has none of those systemic delays.

Speed is not a feature here — it's a consequence of removing every layer of institutional overhead that doesn't serve the user.

Built by Operators.

Not a startup. A tool.
