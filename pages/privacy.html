Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Bitvora, we take your privacy seriously. We operate a completely non-custodial exchange platform designed to minimize data collection and prioritize your security.

1. Data Collection

We only collect the absolute minimum data required to process your transaction and satisfy regulatory banking and UPI gateway requirements. This may include your IP address, selected blockchain transaction hashes, and the destination UPI ID.

2. Non-Custodial Guarantee

Bitvora never holds custody of your digital assets. All transactions are peer-to-contract or peer-to-node executed immediately to your Indian Bank Account. We do not store private keys, recovery phrases, or wallet passwords.

3. Data Retention

Financial transaction metadata is retained firmly on our encrypted private servers strictly for anti-money laundering (AML) compliance and operational redundancy.
